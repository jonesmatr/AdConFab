Your Vision, Our Blueprint

Our design process is deeply collaborative. We take the time to understand your needs, desires, and the unique ways in which you plan to use your converted container. Whether you want a minimalist home office or a fully-equipped commercial space, we translate your vision into a precise and practical design.

Completely Customizable Designs

At AdConFab, the design possibilities are limitless. We pride ourselves on our ability to create completely customizable designs that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Our expert designers work with you to choose the ideal placement of windows, doors, electrical systems, and even the perfect color schemes and finishes.
