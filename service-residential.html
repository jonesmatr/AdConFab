Natural Light and Energy Efficiency

We install thermal windows in our residential conversions, welcoming natural light into the space. Besides adding an airy, spacious feel to the container, these windows also contribute to energy efficiency, helping to regulate temperature and reduce your energy costs.

Exquisite Interior Finishes

To create a truly cozy and inviting atmosphere, we insulate each container with finished drywall and adorn the floors with beautiful hardwood. These finishing touches not only provide insulation but also add an element of style and elegance to your container home. From rustic to modern aesthetics, we can create a look that suits your personal taste and complements your existing home design.
